Section 02 — The Experience

What You'll
Encounter

Four days in the high Sierra. Every element is designed to push your edge and reveal what's underneath.

The Summit

22 miles round trip to 14,505 ft — the highest point in the lower 48. The climb was never just about the mountain. It was about the man you become on the way up. The mountain is a symbol for more: more courage, more truth, more resilience, and more of the man you are capable of becoming. As the climb stretches you physically, it also invites you to rise mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

Group Integration

A guided space to process the experience, distill the lessons, and turn your insight into intention for the life waiting for you at home. What happened on the mountain matters. This is where we make sure it stays with you.

Tea Ceremony

On integration day, we gather in ritual to slow down, reconnect, and honor the journey. Through stillness, reflection, and presence, we ground the experience and make space for what is now unfolding.

Brotherhood

The men you climb with become the men who hold you. This bond doesn't end at the trailhead—it begins there. Shared challenge creates a different kind of connection—one rooted in truth, respect, and real support.
